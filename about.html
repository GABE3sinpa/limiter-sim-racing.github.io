About The Team

Limiter Sim Racing is a two-driver endurance sim racing team competing in iRacing special events with a focus on consistency, strategy, and long-distance race execution.

Team Identity

Built around endurance racing principles, the team prioritizes clean stints, predictable pace, and structured race strategy over short-term performance.

Foundation

Established in 2026, Limiter Sim Racing was created with the goal of building a competitive and disciplined endurance program within iRacing.

Competition Platform

The team competes exclusively in iRacing endurance events, including multi-class special events such as 24-hour races and major endurance series.

Ford Mustang GT3 Program

The Ford Mustang GT3 serves as the primary platform for all endurance entries, chosen for its stability, consistency, and suitability for long stints in traffic-heavy racing.

Operations Approach

Race execution is built around structured stints, driver consistency, and clean traffic management. Strategy decisions are prioritized over aggressive short-term moves.
