About Us

Limiter Sim Racing was founded on January 1, 2026, in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania. Our goal is to climb the ranks in every class we participate in, while maintaining a clean and respectful reputation in the sim racing community.

Our team operates with real-world motorsport principles and professional sim racing hardware. We focus on endurance racing, strategy, and teamwork to push our performance to the limit.

We compete with drivers who share a passion for excellence and sportsmanship, constantly striving to improve lap times and consistency while learning from each event.
